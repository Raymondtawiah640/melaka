Who We Are ▼

Melakah InfoTech Solutions is a premier advertising, technology, and digital marketing powerhouse rooted in the Eastern Region of Ghana. We are a Christ-centered brand, driven by creativity, innovation, and integrity, committed to transforming businesses through cutting-edge digital solutions, impactful brand strategies, and world-class technology services.

About Us ▼

Founded with a vision to blend technology, marketing, and creativity, Melakah InfoTech Solutions empowers brands to reach new heights through modern strategies, innovative technologies, and faith-driven values. We stand at the intersection of advertising excellence and digital innovation, delivering solutions that inspire trust, foster community impact, and glorify God through service.

What We Do ▼

  • Advertising & Branding – Creative advertising campaigns, corporate identity design, brand storytelling, and promotional strategies.
  • Digital Marketing – Social media marketing, SEO, content marketing, influencer partnerships, digital PR, and performance-driven campaigns.
  • Technology Solutions – Website development, app development, software solutions, IT consultancy, and cloud integration.
  • Media & Communications – Video production, photography, copywriting, digital content creation, and strategic communication.
  • Training & Capacity Building – Digital literacy programs, corporate training in IT & marketing, and workshops on branding and innovation.
